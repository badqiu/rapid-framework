
1. 在eclipse中增加avatar-gen要依赖的项目以便生成代码
2. 修改生成器要生成package，直接修改代码: cn.org.rapid_framework.generator.GeneratorMain
3. 以application方式运行GeneratorMain
4. 为项目增加jmock的maven依赖
	<dependency>
	    <groupId>org.jmock</groupId>
  		<artifactId>jmock</artifactId>
  		<version>2.5.1</version>
	</dependency>
	
	<dependency>
	    <groupId>org.jmock</groupId>
  		<artifactId>jmock-junit4</artifactId>
  		<version>2.5.1</version>  		
  	</dependency>
